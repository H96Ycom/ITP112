Frequently Asked Questions


What is NObesity all about?

NObesity's mission is to encourage Singaporeans who are leading a sedentary lifestyle to get active and eat a balanced diet. Obesity rates in Singapore have been rising over the past few years and we hope that this trend will reverse in the near future. Join us and say "NO" to obesity!

What are Healthpoints?

Healthpoints are virtual currency that you earn when using NObesity. These Healthpoints can only be used within the NObesity web app and cannot be withdrawn as cash or any other legal tender currency.

How can I earn Healthpoints?

Healthpoints can be earned through regularly using our NObesity web app. The more often you use NObesity, the more Healthpoints you will get!

How do I get started on NObesity?

To get started with us on NObesity, simply click the "Sign Up" button at the top right of the page and follow the subsequent instructions. You will be prompted to enter your personal health details such as your current weight, height and blood pressure.

What can I use my Healthpoints for?

The Healthpoints you earn through using the NObesity web app can be used to redeem exclusive rewards only given to NObesity members. Please go to the "Rewards" page after you log in to see the list of available rewards you are eligible for.
